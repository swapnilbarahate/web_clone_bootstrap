Are you using robotic process automation in your organization? Congratulations! You’ve taken step one of your business transformation journey.

But do you know how your bots are performing? Are they working effectively, or actually losing you time and money? How can you tell?

If you want to truly succeed in optimizing your business, you need the right robots, precisely calibrated.

You need Pointee.

IMPROVING EFFICIENCY

Pointee analyses your existing robotic processes to ensure they are running as efficiently as possible.

Using data from your RPA platform, the leading software program can see precisely what your robots are up to.

Within a few minutes, it can wade through large amounts of complex data and turn out easy-to-read graphs with actionable insights and recommendations for potential business savings or optimization opportunities.
