Solve your voice AI challenges with Curious Thing

Robotic process automation, or RPA, typically requires structured data for efficient operation. Yet 80% of enterprise data is buried in unstructured documents – think emails, invoices and receipts.

Voice and call-back processes that require human subjectivity only add to the limitations.

Curious Thing is a voice AI tool with the smarts to structure data and iron out any potential issues posed by human subjectivity, greatly increasing the efficacy of voice AI.

It’s a win for organisations keen to embark on their RPA journey, with minimum hassle en route!

WHY VOICE AI?

IProgrammable voice AI allows human interaction in a simple phone call, ensuring you keep your automated processes flying.

The future will see increasingly effective applications of RPA Voice AI technology, in the form of conversational RPA services – harnessing the power of natural language and understanding, for more accurate interpretation.

Where natural language and machine learning intersect in ever more intelligent ways, we can look forward to genuinely end-to-end automation of customer or employee-facing processes.

WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

  • Accelerate customer-employee communication
  • Shrink lead time
  • Expand your operations
  • Reduce cost to serve
  • Ensure consistent experience, any time of day.
