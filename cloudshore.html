Setting you up
for success

Are you looking to automate processes within SAP, but find the software's richness and complexity challenging?

That’s where CloudShore comes in – a tool specifically designed to automate processes within SAP using by your existing Robotic Process Automation Software.

Our pre-configured automations and AI components are tailor-made for SAP, ensuring faster adoption of automation while complying with SAP guidelines. With CloudShore, you can identify automation opportunities, customize bots to meet your requirements, and seamlessly deploy them into the RPA orchestrator of your choice.

It’s simple: select the right bots, train them, and run them. Let CloudShore handle the complexity, so you can focus on what really matters – optimizing your SAP processes.

Cloudshore comes with two main features –AI Center and SAP Actions

CLOUDSHORE FOR YOUR SAP AUTOMATION

If you’re looking to increase your organization’s Automation and AI execution capacity, look no further – with CloudShore’s standardized actions, scaling RPA becomes fast and simple.

Are you struggling to identify the right automation opportunities? Or finding it challenging to bring together the necessary experts for comprehensive automation? CloudShore has you covered.

When it comes to automation portability and SAP licensing policies, you can trust CloudShore to ensure compliance and seamless integration. We’ve solved all of these challenges, so you don’t have to.

With CloudShore’s SAP Actions, you can expect outstanding performance, compliance, scalability, and faster deployment.

Our actions interact with SAP through BAPIs, providing a 10x boost in speed compared to conventional automation methods. Plus, we use SAP’s native BAPIs, ensuring full portability during your SAP upgrades and compliance with SAP’s licensing policies.

We’ve also integrated with the most common technologies that integrate with SAP, providing scalability and flexibility.

When it comes to deployment, you’ll be pleased to know that we offer over 160 actions, including the 80% most frequently used in RPA implementations over SAP ECC/S4HANA. This means you can start automating right away with our ready-to-use actions.

CLOUDSHORE'S AI CENTER

Unlock the potential of AI in your processes with CloudShore’s pre-built Machine Learning algorithms.

With our easy-to-follow process, you can teach your AI components to differentiate regular and irregular transactions in just four simple steps:

  1. Choose the algorithm that best fits your needs.
  2. Download and complete our training file templates.
  3. Upload your completed training file.
  4. Train your AI model and download the results.

It’s that easy! With CloudShore’s Machine Learning algorithms and simple training process, you can bring AI to your business and start seeing the benefits in no time.
