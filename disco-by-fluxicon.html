What is your data really trying to tell you?

It’s not enough to just collect and store data anymore. Intelligent interpretation is vital if you want to make waves in your industry.

Disco by Fluxicon is data interpretation software at the top of its game. Providing a comprehensive review of your current processes based on your business’ data footprint, Disco collates and analyzes your data so you can see an accurate picture of your business and plan your next move.

This user-friendly desktop software was designed to help you streamline and manage complex processes often gleaned through CRMs, ERPs or Excel spreadsheets, so it’s data you already have stored.

Simple to use, yet highly sophisticated in application, Disco by Fluxicon makes process mining next-level fast, easy and highly efficient.

STATE-OF-THE-ART PROCESS MINING

As leading providers of exceptional process mining technology, Fluxicon experts have been honing the Disco model for more than a decade.

After studying more than a hundred businesses across multiple industries and sectors, they developed Disco for local or virtual application.

The popular process mining software toolkit visualizes data, correlating it to create an in-depth process analysis, allowing objective data interpretation and ruling out assumptions.
