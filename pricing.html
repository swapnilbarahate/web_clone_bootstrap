PRICING

Pay. Your way.

We have two pricing models designed to give you choice and flexibility when accessing the Automatorr platform.

1. SELF-SERVICE

If you’d prefer to pay a flat rate, the self-service payment method might be right for you.

When using the platform and paying self-service:

  • clients pay an 11% service fee on the total project fee awarded to a freelancer.clients pay an 11% service fee on the total project fee awarded to a freelancer.
  • freelancers pay a 5% service fee on the total project fee they receive from a client.

2. SUBSCRIPTION

A subscription fee of $450 per month (with no contractual or annual commitments) gives you access to an exclusive pool of top automation talent (TAT). These highly skilled TAT professionals are only available to subscribers and are ready to work for you within 48 hours.

For every freelancer hired under the subscription model, you can expect to pay project fees ranging from $90 per hour to $150 per hour.

Keen to know more about our payment options? Contact us today.

*Please note: All pricing is subject to change without notice. Of course, we do honour any hourly pricing that is agreed to before a job is awarded to a freelancer.
