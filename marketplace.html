HOW IT WORKS

Our platform is a one-stop shop for organizations and automation experts to meet, greet and do business, so the whole process is simple and intuitive.

Before coming on board, Automatorr vets all our freelance operators, so you can be sure the people you employ have the skills and experience you need for the project at hand.

If you’re a business or individual, you can post your project (listing the kind of assistance or skill set you require), and professionals from our pool of freelancers bid on the job.

The successful bidder/s are then awarded the work by the business or individual who posted the listed, and Automatorr will charge up to 16% of the project cost for facilitating the process and transaction.
